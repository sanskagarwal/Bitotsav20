About

Bitotsav'20 brings you a prodigious opportunity to not only be a part of this whimsical journey but also represent your institute in the fest. Student Ambassador Program (SAP) provides you with just the suitable platform to put your leadership, management and communication skills to test. From exploring infinitesimal possibilities and enhancing your team-building skills to joining forces with creative minds to conquer the odds, SAP is a guaranteed way of surpassing the boundaries of excellence. Do you have what it takes to outshine the competition and take home the coveted prize? So come along on this momentous experience as a Student Ambassador to lead your team to unremitting glory.

F.A.Q

  • Who can become a College Ambassador?

    Any College Student with a valid College Student Identity Card.

  • How do I apply?

    You can apply directly through our website.

  • How many College Ambassador can be there from a college?

    Can appoint more than one College Ambassador in the same college if the student participation from their college is large enough.

  • What is my responsibility being a part of the College Ambassador program?

    Work actively for social media publicity Strategize and drive Word of Mouth publicity campaigns in campus events and activities

  • What happens after I apply?

    Once the selection process is completed, you will get notified if you are selected.

  • Who can I contact if I have any questions regarding the College Ambassador program?

    You can contact us at info@bitotsav.in
